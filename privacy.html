Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 February 2026

Who We Are

Jaystack is a business consulting partnership headquartered in Perth, Western Australia. We provide bill auditing, cost analysis, website development, content creation, and business automation services to clients worldwide across all industries.

What Data We Collect

  • Contact information you provide: name, email, phone number, business name, address
  • Documents you provide for analysis: bills, invoices, statements, contracts
  • Data we extract during analysis: provider names, plan details, amounts, usage quantities, rates, and dates
  • Information submitted through our website contact form

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Credit card or bank account numbers (payments processed securely through Stripe)
  • Government-issued ID numbers
  • Health or medical information
  • Data not relevant to the services you requested

How We Use Your Data

  • To perform the analysis, consulting, or development services you requested
  • To generate reports and recommendations
  • To communicate with you about your service

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

AI Processing

We use AI technology to assist with data extraction and analysis. When processing your documents:

  • Document content is sent to AI APIs (Anthropic Claude and/or Google Gemini) for processing
  • These services operate zero-retention policies for API usage — your data is not stored by them or used for AI model training
  • Analysis results are stored only in our secure systems

How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored in encrypted cloud services (Google Drive, Notion) with access restricted to the Jaystack team. No third parties have access to your raw data.

Data Retention

  • Active clients: data retained for the duration of service plus 12 months
  • After service ends: data retained for 12 months, then permanently deleted
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time

Your Rights

Regardless of your location, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format

For Australian residents: protected under the Australian Privacy Act 1988. For UK/EU residents: protected under UK GDPR / EU GDPR. For California residents: protected under the CCPA.

To exercise any rights, email hello@jaystack.com.au.

International Data Transfers

Jaystack is based in Australia. If you are outside Australia, your data may be transferred to and processed in Australia. We ensure your data is protected to the same standard regardless of location.

Data Breaches

In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected clients within 48 hours and report to relevant authorities as required.

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

Changes

We may update this policy. Active clients will be notified of material changes.
